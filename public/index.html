DESCRIPTION
HexTank.io is a play-to-earn Web3 instant game built on the Nimiq blockchain.
The action takes place in a PVP sci-fi battle arena. You are in charge of a HexTank. Your job is to eliminate all other players and climb to the top of the score leaderboard.
HexTank.io is available on all major browsers, including desktop and mobile versions.
GAME RULES
You can play HexTank.io in two modes: free and paid with Nimiq. Each HexTank gets 5 health points when joining a continuous deathmatch tournament.
In the free mode, you fight for the #1 place on the score leaderboard. You will rank based on the total damage that you have given.
In the paid mode, you will join a continuous deathmatch tournament with a 500 NIM entry fee. 90% of the fee goes to the prize pool. For a complete kill of your enemy, you earn 450 NIM. For each dealt hit, you get 90 NIM. The HexTank with the most NIM earned gets all the glory on the score leaderboard!
CONTROLS
DESKTOP
KEYBOARD: WASD or ARROW KEYS for movement, SPACE for shooting.
GAMEPAD/JOYSTICK: THUMB STICKS for movement, L1/L2/R1/R2 for shooting.
MOBILE
TOUCH CONTROLS: ARROWS for movement, RED CIRCLE for shooting.

FAQ
#1 Can I play HexTank.io without a Nimiq wallet?
Yes, you can try the free mode.
#2 Can I play HexTank.io if I own another cryptocurrency instead of NIM?
Yes, use your Nimiq wallet or a non-custodial exchange to convert your current cryptocurrency to NIM.
#3 The current tournament that I joined looks empty. What can I do?
HexTank.io doesn't use any bots. All players are real players. Feel free to invite some friends and spread awareness about HexTank.io in order to gain more players for the current tournament!
#4 I'm experiencing connectivity issues. What can I do?
Ensure that you are playing using a stable Wi-Fi or ethernet connection. 5G networks or lower might not provide the best stability. Be sure that you are playing HexTank.io in the closest region near you.
#5 What happens if I accidentally close the window, if I get disconnected or if I want a refund?
HexTank.io will automatically refund 90% of your current entry fee left in your balance to your Nimiq wallet. If you want a refund, you can close the current game window.
#6 Does HexTank.io hold any funds?
HexTank.io is a non-custodial game, meaning it doesn't hold any player funds besides the prize pool during the game session. Each amount of NIM you earn gets sent instantly to your Nimiq wallet.
#7 Does HexTank.io collect any user-identifiable data?
No, HexTank.io doesn't collect any user-identifiable data such as IP addresses and it doesn't use cookies.
